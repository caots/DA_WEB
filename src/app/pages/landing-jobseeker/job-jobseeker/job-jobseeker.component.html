Step 1:

Search For Jobs

Sick of job search engines full of irrelevant clutter? Our simplified job search engine is the place to start your journey to your dream job.

Step 2:

Create A Profile

Create a MeasuredSkills jobseeker profile for free to apply for jobs, take skills assessments, save jobs, follow employers, and more. Not a bad deal, right?

Step 3:

Take Assessments

Now's your chance to flex your skills. Break free from a traditional resume and build a profile that showcases exactly what you bring to the team. Take skills assessments required by job listings or proactively show your potential by taking assessments on your own time.

Step 4:

Apply for Jobs

Take all of your hand-earned skills and awesome qualities, and start applying for jobs. A potential employer may reach out and ask you to take further skill assessments. Good luck; you got this!

Step 5:

Message Employers

Once you’ve applied for a job, it’s now the employer’s turn to reach out and message you through our messaging portal. Once the employer messages you, you can share additional information about yourself and send supporting materials. Remember, your last name and profile picture will remain masked until you’re ready to share your full identity with the employer. Once you share your contact information, employers are free to setup interviews with you outside of MeasuredSkills. We think this semi-anonymous application process will break the ice, reduce hiring bias, and help you land your next job.

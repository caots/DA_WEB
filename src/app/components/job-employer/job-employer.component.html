Step 1:

Post a Job

In addition to including typical details about the job role and responsibilities, let applicants know what skills you are looking for by linking skills assessments to your job post. To apply, jobseekers will take these assessments, so you have a better picture of their skills.

Step 2:

Jobseekers Search Jobs & Apply

When a jobseeker is interested in applying for your job, they will take the required assessments that you have specified. If they have taken an assessment before, their old score will be carried over automatically.

In addition, you can search for potential candidates by assessment score, location, and asking salary using the “Find Candidates” feature. You can then invite them to apply to one of your active job posts or direct message them through our messaging portal for an additional fee.

Step 3:

Message Applicants

Reach out to applicants using our messaging portal and start a conversation to narrow your list down to the best prospective candidates. You can ask applicants questions, request supporting materials, rate them, take notes, and track their interview status. Applicants’ last names and profile pictures will remain masked until they’re ready to share their full identity with you. Once they share their contact information, you are free to setup interviews outside of MeasuredSkills. We think this semi-anonymous application process will break the ice, reduce hiring bias, and help you find the right person for your job.

Step 4:

Select Your Hire

It’s time to celebrate! You can be confident when you make your hire that they are the right person for your job.

Spotlight Feature

Track Your Stats and Hiring Funnel

Under “Account Statistics”, track your total views, follower count, and your recruitment funnel. You can use our recruitment funnel tool to gain insights into your hiring efficiency on MeasuredSkills. Examine your overall totals, per job average, and stats for each specific job posted.

Most Freqently Asked Questions

If I take an assessment, can I use it for multiple job applications?

  • Yes. That’s a big benefit of our system. If you’ve taken an assessment for one job, the score will be automatically carried over for other jobs that require the same assessment. We want to save you time and make the application process easy.

How many times can I take an assessment?

  • You can attempt each catalog assessment twice for free. After that, you can earn free retake credits by referring friends or purchase additional credits. This is to prevent excessive guessing on assessments. You can only take an employer’s custom assessment once when you apply. These are not accessible via the catalog. You will have the option to retake an employer’s custom assessment after 30 days if you apply to another job that uses the same assessment.

If I’ve taken an assessment multiple times, which score counts?

  • Only the highest score(s) for each assessment is calculated in your overall application score and visible to employers. Only employers can see the specific weighting of each assessment and your overall weighted score. This is unique to each job. You can view all your past scores in your score history.

Can I take an assessment without applying to a job?

  • Yes, you can proactively take assessments under the Assessments tab.

Can employers find me?

  • Once you’ve taken at least one (1) assessment, employers will be able to find you under Find Candidates. They can then invite you to apply to any of their active jobs or direct message you. Your name and profile photo still remain anonymous until you reveal that information to them in Messages. You also have the choice to show employers your location, minimum salary requirement, or opt- out of this feature entirely in the Find Candidates tab in your profile settings.

What does this icon mean?

  • This is the “Responsive User” badge. We want to encourage timely replies by employers and jobseekers and discourage ghosting. Employers and jobseekers who are prompt at replying to their messages are rewarded with this badge which is displayed on their respective profile cards.

How can I contact MeasuredSkills?

  • Please visit our Contact Us page to get in touch. If you have an existing account, you can also message us through Messages by clicking on  Contact MeasuredSkills . If you have any questions, need assistance with our platform, or would like to provide feedback or report bugs we would be happy to hear from you.
